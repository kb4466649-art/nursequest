This Privacy Policy explains how NurseQuest ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website and application. By using NurseQuest, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: If you sign in with Google or email, we receive your name, email address, profile picture, and a unique user ID.
  • Usage data: Questions attempted, scores, accuracy, time spent, and progress within the app, used to power features like progress tracking and rankings.
  • User-submitted content: Questions, PDFs, images, or text you choose to upload or generate through the app's practice and scanning tools.
  • Device and log data: Basic technical data such as browser type, device type, and IP address, collected automatically for security and performance.

Guests who use the app without signing in are not linked to a personal account, though limited local/session data may still be used to run the app.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core features: quizzes, mock tests, progress tracking, and rankings.
  • To personalize your experience and save your progress across sessions.
  • To communicate app updates, reminders, or notifications you opt into.
  • To maintain and improve app performance, security, and content quality.

3. Third-Party Services

NurseQuest uses trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Google Sign-In / Firebase: for authentication and data storage.
  • Google AdSense: to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other sites. You can learn more and manage ad personalization at google.com/settings/ads.
  • AI providers (such as Google Gemini, Groq, OpenRouter, or similar): used to generate or verify practice questions. Content you submit for question generation may be processed by these providers according to their own privacy terms.
  • Push notification providers (such as OneSignal): used only if you opt in to notifications.

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

4. Cookies

We and our advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to remember preferences, keep you signed in, and show relevant ads. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work correctly if you do.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers listed above solely to operate the app, or when required by law.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices

  • You may use the app as a guest without creating an account.
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising through your Google Account settings.

8. Children's Privacy

NurseQuest is intended for exam candidates and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
